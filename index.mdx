## How Nexu works

Nexu runs a local controller on your machine that compiles an AI Agent configuration, connects it to your chosen messaging channels, and keeps everything running in the background. Your Agent processes messages through the OpenClaw runtime and responds — no cloud middleman, no vendor lock-in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and install">
    Grab the installer for macOS (Apple Silicon or Intel) or Windows from [nexu.io](https://nexu.io). Double-click to install — no terminal required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a model">
    Sign in with your Nexu account for instant access to managed models, or paste your own API key from Anthropic, OpenAI, Google AI, or any OpenAI-compatible provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a channel">
    Pick Feishu, Slack, Discord, WeChat, or another supported platform and complete the short setup flow in the Nexu dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Open your connected app on any device and talk to your Agent. It's online as long as Nexu is running on your desktop.
  </Step>
</Steps>
