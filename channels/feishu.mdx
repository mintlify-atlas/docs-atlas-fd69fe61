---
title: "Connect Nexu to Feishu (Lark)"
description: "Use your Feishu App ID and App Secret to connect your OpenClaw agent to Feishu. No public server needed — Nexu uses long-connection WebSocket mode."
---

Nexu connects to Feishu through a custom app you create on the Feishu Open Platform. All you need are two values — an **App ID** and an **App Secret** — and the full setup takes about 10 minutes. Nexu uses Feishu's long-connection (WebSocket) mode, so no public IP address or callback URL is required.

## Prerequisites

- A [Feishu](https://www.feishu.cn) account (personal or enterprise)
- Access to the [Feishu Open Platform](https://open.feishu.cn/app) — sign in with the same account
- Nexu installed on your Mac ([download](https://nexu.io))

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Feishu app">
    1. Go to the [Feishu Open Platform](https://open.feishu.cn/app), sign in, and click **Create Custom App**.
    2. Fill in the app name, description, and icon, then click **Create**.
    3. On the **Credentials & Basic Info** page, copy both values and keep them ready:
       - **App ID**
       - **App Secret**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add credentials to Nexu">
    Open the Nexu client, navigate to **Channels → Feishu**, paste your App ID and App Secret into the fields, and click **Connect**.

    Nexu immediately validates the credentials against the Feishu API. If validation succeeds, the channel status changes to **Connected**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure events and callbacks">
    Nexu uses Feishu's long-connection mode to receive events, so no webhook URL is needed.

    **Event configuration:**

    1. In your app, go to **Events & Callbacks → Event Configuration**.
    2. Click the edit button next to **Subscription Method**, select **Use Long Connection to Receive Events**, and click **Save**.
    3. Click **Add Event** and add all four of the following events:
       - `p2p_chat_create` — First conversation created between user and bot
       - `im.message.receive_v1` — Receive message
       - `im.chat.member.bot.added_v1` — Bot added to group
       - `im.chat.access_event.bot_p2p_chat_entered_v1` — User enters bot conversation

    **Callback configuration:**

    1. Switch to the **Callback Configuration** tab.
    2. Click the edit button next to **Subscription Method**, select **Use Long Connection to Receive Callbacks**, and click **Save**.
    3. Click **Add Callback**, select the **Card** category, check **Card Action Trigger** (`card.action.trigger`), and click **Add**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish the app and verify">
    1. In the Feishu Open Platform, go to **Version Management & Release**.
    2. Click **Create Version**, fill in the version number and release notes, and click **Save**.
    3. Click **Publish** and wait for approval (usually instant for internal apps).
    4. Once approved, click **Chat** in the Nexu client to open a conversation with your bot in Feishu.
  </Step>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Credentials are invalid (409 error)">
    Double-check that you copied the **App ID** and **App Secret** from the **Credentials & Basic Info** page of the correct app. Ensure there are no leading or trailing spaces when pasting. If the App Secret has been rotated, generate a new one and reconnect in Nexu.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The bot does not reply to messages">
    The most common causes are:

    - **Events not configured**: Make sure all four events are added under Event Configuration and the subscription method is set to Long Connection.
    - **App not published**: The app must have a published version before it can receive live traffic.
    - **Nexu not running**: The Nexu client must be running in the background for your agent to receive and reply to messages.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Permissions import shows fewer than 178 scopes">
    Some scopes may already be present or unavailable for your account type. After import, click **Apply** regardless — the system will only add the delta. Enterprise accounts may need admin approval for certain scopes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need a public server or callback URL?">
    No. Nexu uses Feishu's long-connection (WebSocket) mode throughout — for both events and callbacks. No public IP address or externally reachable URL is required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Card actions (buttons) do not work">
    Ensure the `card.action.trigger` callback is added under Callback Configuration, and that the subscription method is also set to Long Connection. This is separate from the event configuration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
