title: "Connect Nexu to WeChat via OpenClaw"
description: "Scan a QR code to connect your personal WeChat account to Nexu using the OpenClaw ClawBot plugin. Requires WeChat 8.0.7+ on macOS 12+ (Apple Silicon)."
---

Nexu connects to WeChat through the **OpenClaw ClawBot plugin**, which is built into WeChat 8.0.7 and later. The entire setup is a single QR-code scan — no WeChat Work account, no Official Account, and no public server required. The process takes under 5 minutes.

<Warning>
  **WeChat 8.0.7 or later is required.** Older versions of WeChat do not include the ClawBot plugin that Nexu depends on. Update WeChat before proceeding.
</Warning>

## Prerequisites

- **WeChat** version 8.0.7 or later (check via WeChat → About)
- **macOS 12+** on Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3)
- Nexu installed ([download at nexu.io](https://nexu.io))

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Update WeChat to 8.0.7 or later">
    Open WeChat on your Mac and update to version 8.0.7 or later. This is the minimum version that includes the ClawBot plugin Nexu uses to receive messages.

    To check your version: open WeChat → menu bar → **WeChat → About WeChat**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in to Nexu">
    Open Nexu from your Applications folder. On the welcome screen, choose a sign-in method:

    - **Use your Nexu account** (recommended) — sign in to get access to Claude, GPT, Gemini, and other top models.
    - **Use your own models (BYOK)** — enter your own API key; no Nexu account required.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the WeChat channel">
    On the Nexu home screen, click **WeChat** in the **Choose a channel to get started** section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scan to connect WeChat">
    1. When the **Connect WeChat** dialog appears, click the green **Scan to Connect** button.
    2. Nexu automatically installs the WeChat ClawBot plugin and generates a QR code. The screen shows **Waiting for scan…**
    3. Open WeChat on your **phone**, tap **Scan** (or use the QR scanner in the WeChat search bar), and scan the QR code displayed on your Mac screen.
    4. Tap **Confirm** on your phone to authorize the connection.

    <Note>
      The QR code is generated fresh each time. If it expires before you scan it, click **Scan to Connect** again to get a new one.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    After you confirm on your phone, the WeChat channel on the Nexu home screen shows a **Connected** status. The connection is now live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Chatting with your agent on mobile

Once connected, open WeChat on your phone (or Mac). You will see a conversation named **WeChat ClawBot** in your chat list. Send any message to that conversation to start chatting with your OpenClaw agent.

<Tip>
  The WeChat ClawBot conversation is available on both your phone and Mac. After the initial QR scan on your computer, you can chat with your agent entirely from your phone — no desktop interaction needed for ongoing use.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need WeChat Work or an Official Account?">
    No. The ClawBot plugin works with a personal WeChat account (version 8.0.7+). WeChat Work and Official Accounts are separate products and are not required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will my WeChat account get banned?">
    No. ClawBot is an official WeChat plugin built into the app. Using it is fully compliant with WeChat's terms of service.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need a public server or callback URL?">
    No. Nexu connects directly through the WeChat ClawBot plugin using a local connection. No public IP address or webhook is required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can the agent reply when my Mac is asleep or off?">
    The Nexu client must be running and your Mac must not be asleep. As long as Nexu is active in the background, your agent can reply to WeChat messages 24/7.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I connect WeChat and other channels at the same time?">
    Yes. Nexu supports simultaneous connections to WeChat, Feishu, Slack, Discord, and other channels. Your agent responds across all connected platforms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I switch the AI model used in WeChat?">
    Use the model selector at the top of the Nexu home screen to switch between Claude, GPT, Gemini, and other models. The change applies to all connected channels immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
