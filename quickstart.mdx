---
title: "Get started with Nexu in under five minutes"
description: "Download Nexu, choose a model, connect a messaging channel, and send your first message to your AI Agent — all without touching a terminal."
---

This guide walks you through everything from downloading the Nexu desktop app to sending your first message to your AI Agent inside a real messaging platform. The whole flow takes under five minutes for most users. By the end you'll have a running Agent reachable from your phone via Feishu, Slack, Discord, or WeChat.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and install Nexu">
    Download the installer for your platform from [nexu.io](https://nexu.io) or from the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/nexu-io/nexu/releases).

    | Platform | Download |
    |---|---|
    | macOS (Apple Silicon) | [nexu.io](https://nexu.io) · [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/nexu-io/nexu/releases) |
    | macOS (Intel) | [nexu.io](https://nexu.io) · [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/nexu-io/nexu/releases) |
    | Windows | [nexu.io](https://nexu.io) · [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/nexu-io/nexu/releases) |

    Once downloaded, double-click the installer. No terminal, no environment variables, no dependency setup — Nexu is ready to use as soon as installation finishes.

    <Info>
      **System requirements:** macOS 12+ (Apple Silicon or Intel) or Windows 10+. Approximately 500 MB of free storage.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a model on first launch">
    When Nexu opens for the first time, click **Settings** in the left sidebar to open the AI Model Providers page. You have two options:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Nexu Official (recommended for new users)">
        Select **Nexu Official** from the provider list, then click **Sign in to Nexu**.

        Once signed in, high-quality managed models are available immediately — no API key required. Use the **Nexu Bot Model** dropdown at the top of Settings to pick the active model.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="BYOK — Bring Your Own Key">
        Select a provider from the list — **Anthropic**, **OpenAI**, **Google AI**, **xAI**, or **Custom** for any OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

        1. Paste your API key into the **API Key** field.
        2. Optionally set an **API Proxy URL** if you need a custom gateway.
        3. Click **Save**. Nexu verifies the key and loads your available models automatically.

        You can configure multiple BYOK providers at the same time and switch between them using the model selector without affecting any existing conversations.

        <Tip>
          MiniMax, OpenAI Codex, and GLM (Z.AI Coding Plan) support OAuth login — click **Authorize** instead of pasting a key. See [OAuth models](/models/oauth) for details.
        </Tip>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect a messaging channel">
    With a model configured, pick a channel to connect your Agent to. Each channel has a short setup flow inside the Nexu dashboard.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Feishu">
        Feishu is the quickest channel to set up — you only need an **App ID** and **App Secret** from your Feishu developer console.

        See the full [Feishu setup guide](/channels/feishu) for step-by-step instructions.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Slack">
        Nexu generates a Slack app manifest for you. Paste it into the Slack App Directory to create your app in one step, then authorize it in your workspace.

        See the full [Slack setup guide](/channels/slack) for step-by-step instructions.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Discord">
        You'll need an **Application ID** and **Bot Token** from the Discord Developer Portal. Nexu walks you through the required bot permissions.

        See the full [Discord setup guide](/channels/discord) for step-by-step instructions.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="WeChat">
        Nexu works with the WeChat 8.0.7 OpenClaw plugin. Click **Connect** inside Nexu, then scan the QR code with the WeChat app on your phone.

        See the full [WeChat setup guide](/channels/wechat) for step-by-step instructions.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    Once the channel credentials are saved, Nexu recompiles the Agent configuration and the OpenClaw runtime reloads automatically. No restart needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Click **Chat** in the Nexu sidebar to open a conversation and verify your Agent responds correctly before switching to your phone.

    Then open the messaging app you connected — Feishu, Slack, Discord, or WeChat — and send a message to your Agent. As long as Nexu is running on your desktop, your Agent is online and reachable from any device.

    <Tip>
      Want to give your Agent extra capabilities? Browse the [Skills catalog](/skills/catalog) to install one-click skills for Feishu Bitable, Docs, Calendar, and more, or read [Custom skills](/skills/custom) to build your own.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
